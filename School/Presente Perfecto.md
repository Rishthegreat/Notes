# Presente Perfecto
---

- To describe events that have not occurred recently or “up to the present” (yet)
- “Has happened”
	- First part of the word “has” is conjugated and “happened” is the the past participle
- Not yet in spanish
	- Todavía
	- Aún no
- Últimamente →Lately
- Reaentemente →Recently
- Conjugation of Haber
